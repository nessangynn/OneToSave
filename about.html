About

OneToSave allows users to be informed on the current catastrophes and issues that present challenges to society, as well as connecting them to the organizations that support and are involved in the relief efforts for those disasters and problems. Unlike other donation websites, OneToSave highlights various organizations and recent catastrophes instead of having one single focal point. This application showcases important news in a simple accessible way, one does not need to visit multiple websites to gain information or to donate to different causes.



Our Mission

This application is unique as it presents current events as well as charitable organizations, that have been verified, that link to those events. This application saves users time as it gives users a direct connection to verified charities and organizations so that users do not need to filter through a list themselves.
